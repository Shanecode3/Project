Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TailorMyLetter. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Resume & Job Description: Uploaded data is used solely for generating a personalized cover letter and ATS feedback. We do not store or share your files.
  • Device & Usage Info: We may collect limited analytics data (e.g., browser type, screen size) to improve user experience.
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase, your payment is processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your uploaded resume and job description are processed temporarily by our AI model to generate your output. No personally identifiable information is retained or reused.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies minimally for remembering your theme preferences (light/dark mode) and session-based UI enhancements. We do not track or serve ads.

5. Data Security

All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. Files are processed in-memory and are not stored on our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

We use OpenRouter AI for generating cover letters and Stripe for secure payments. Both services are GDPR and PCI-DSS compliant respectively.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with the revised date.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, please contact us at: tailormyletter@gmail.com
